# Scrum Guide for Api_Weather

## Daily Scrum
- The Daily Scrum is a short, focused meeting for the development team.
- Duration: **Maximum 15 minutes**.
- Purpose: To synchronize activities, identify blockers, and plan work for the next 24 hours.
- Conducted at the same time and place each day.

## Roles

### Product Owner
- Responsible for maximizing the value of the product.
- Manages the product backlog and ensures the team understands priorities.

### Backend Developers (2)
- Develop and maintain the server-side logic, APIs, and database interactions.
- Collaborate with the tester and product owner to ensure quality and functionality.

### Tester
- Ensures that the product meets quality standards.
- Performs manual and/or automated testing, reports bugs, and verifies fixes.

## Other Scrum Events
### Sprint Planning
- Defines the sprint goal and selects backlog items for the sprint.

### Sprint Review
- Demonstrates the completed work to stakeholders and gathers feedback.

### Sprint Retrospective
- Reflects on the previous sprint to identify improvements for the next one.
